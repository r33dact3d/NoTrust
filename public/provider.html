How It Works for Service Providers

Powered by BSV, MNEE & RockWallet

Here’s how you, as a Service Provider, can use NoTrust to sell services securely:

How Do I Get Started?

Create a listing with your BSV wallet, a detailed service description, price in MNEE, and delivery timeframe. You’ll lock a 5K MNEE arbitration fee to ensure commitment.

What Happens After I List My Service?

Share your unique Job ID (e.g., NTX-12345) with a buyer. They’ll fund escrow with your price plus a 25K MNEE fee (20K for NoTrust, 5K for arbitration).

How Do I Get Paid?

Deliver your service within the specified days. If the buyer approves (or doesn’t dispute within 24 hours), you receive your payment (e.g., 50 MNEE) and your 5K MNEE fee back. NoTrust keeps 20K MNEE.

What If There’s a Dispute?

If the buyer disputes within 24 hours, we review your delivery against the specs:
- You Win: You get your payment (e.g., 50 MNEE) + 5K MNEE back, buyer loses 5K, NoTrust gets 15K MNEE.
- Buyer Wins: Buyer gets a full refund + 5K MNEE, you lose 5K, NoTrust gets 15K MNEE.

How Do I Avoid Problems?

- Use precise specs (e.g., “JPG, 300 DPI,” not “pretty”).
- Deliver within your set days—missed deadlines mean automatic loss.
- Upload proof (e.g., file hash) to BSV to strengthen your case in disputes.
