How It Works for Prospective Clients

Powered by BSV, MNEE & RockWallet

Here’s how you, as a Prospective Client, can use NoTrust to buy services securely:

How Do I Find a Service?

Browse listings to find a service you need. Each listing shows the seller, description, price, delivery date, and a unique Job ID (e.g., NTX-12345).

How Do I Pay?

Use the Job ID to fund escrow with the listed price plus a 25K MNEE fee (20K for NoTrust, 5K for arbitration). Your funds are locked securely on BSV.

What Happens After I Fund Escrow?

The seller delivers by the listed date. If it meets the specs and you’re happy (or don’t dispute within 24 hours), the seller gets paid, you lose 5K MNEE, and NoTrust keeps 20K MNEE.

What If I’m Not Satisfied?

Dispute within 24 hours of delivery. We check the specs:
- You Win: Full refund (e.g., 50 MNEE) + 5K MNEE back, seller loses 5K, NoTrust gets 15K MNEE.
- Seller Wins: Seller gets payment + 5K MNEE, you lose 5K, NoTrust gets 15K MNEE.

How Do I Protect Myself?

- Check specs before funding—disputes are based on them, not subjective opinions (e.g., “ugly” doesn’t count).
- Dispute within 24 hours if the delivery doesn’t match, or funds release automatically.
