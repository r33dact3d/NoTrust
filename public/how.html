NoTrust: How It Works

NoTrust is a trustless escrow service using Monero Escrow (MNEE), a stablecoin pegged 1:1 to USD.

1. Sellers create a listing with their email, service description, price, and delivery timeframe.

2. Buyers browse listings or receive a listing URL, then fund escrow with MNEE.

3. Once funded, the seller delivers the service within the agreed timeframe.

4. Buyer confirms delivery, releasing funds from escrow to the seller.

Need MNEE? Get started with RockWallet, CoinEx, or Changelly.
